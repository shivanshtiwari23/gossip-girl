what is this?

A fan-made anonymous imageboard inspired by the Gossip Girl TV show. No accounts. No usernames. No identity. Just gossip.

what can you do here?

Create gossip blasts — pick a photo, write a headline, spill the tea — and post them anonymously to the feed. Browse what others have posted. Comment. Judge. Repeat.

how does it work?

Hit Send a Tip on the home page to create your gossip card. Once you're happy with it, hit the upload button to post it to the feed. That's it. No sign up required.

the rules

Keep it fictional. Keep it fun. This is inspired by a TV show — nobody real should be targeted. Gossip Girl was scandalous but never cruel.

who made this?

A fan project built with pure HTML, CSS and vanilla JavaScript on the frontend — Spring Boot and MySQL on the backend. No frameworks. No fluff. Just vibes and code.
